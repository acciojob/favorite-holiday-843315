Paris, France


Paris is a beautiful city known for its rich history, iconic landmarks, art museums, and romantic atmosphere. It is one of the most popular travel destinations in the world.

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most famous landmark of Paris
  2. Louvre Museum – home to world-famous artworks
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a historic architectural masterpiece
  4. Champs-Élysées – a famous avenue for shopping and walking
  5. Montmartre – known for its artistic charm
  • Croissant – a classic French breakfast pastry
  • Macarons – colorful and delicious desserts
  • Crêpes – sweet or savory street food
  • French Cheese – rich and flavorful varieties
  • Baguette – a traditional French bread
